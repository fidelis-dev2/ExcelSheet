Formula zinazotumika

  • Hesabu ya kawaida: =A1+B1*2
  • Jumla: =SUM(A1:A10)
  • Wastani: =AVERAGE(B2:B20)
  • Kiwango cha chini: =MIN(C1:C8)
  • Kiwango cha juu: =MAX(C1:C8)
  • Kuhesabu namba: =COUNT(D1:D30)
  • Round: =ROUND(A1/3,2)
  • Condition: =IF(A1>=50,1,0)

Anza formula yoyote kwa alama ya =. Mfumo pia unatambua absolute references kama $A$1.
